We Cook With Heart

Each spice has been measured and blended to perfection; the dishes carefully prepared so that no ingredient is left out of balance. The flavour and ambiance constructed so that no grain of rice or slab of tile feels out of place. Each detail has been pored over and thought about so that, while you are here, you can be transported back home to Karachi and yet, still experience something new and unique. With authentic Karahi & Barbeque recipes from around Pakistan it is with great pride that we put our food on plates for you in our restaurant.
