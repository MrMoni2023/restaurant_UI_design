Ambience & Authenticity



Karachi was originally name “Kolachi”. It used to be called the “City of Lights” and is still known for its vibrant nightlife, immense sense of culture, and delicious food. MyKolachi Karahi & Barbeque House represents a snapshot of the light, life, and vibrancy of Karachi — the soul of Pakistan — the vibrance, the sea, the city, MyKolachi!


 

Our Karahi



Our specialty dish is cooked in proper karahi pots that are specially shaped with that signature rounded “wok” style to maintain an even cooking temperature. Our chefs have perfected the technique of cooking in karahis so that you can enjoy each of these recipes as if you were eating them fresh, back home. Cooked fresh while you wait, our key dish is known for its fresh (never frozen) ingredients— just like home.
